Grayout enforces grayscale mode on your Android device to help you reduce phone usage. A colorless screen is less engaging, which makes it easier to put your phone down.

Features:
- Toggle grayscale on and off with a single tap
- Quick Settings tiles for fast access
- Automatic enforcement on a timer so grayscale turns back on after you disable it
- Per-app exclusions so specific apps (maps, photos) stay in color
- Schedule grayscale for specific days and time windows
- Runs as a lightweight foreground service

Setup requires a one-time ADB command to grant the secure settings permission. No root needed.

Grayout is free, open source, and contains no ads or tracking.
